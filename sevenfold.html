F1

Overview:

I am absolutely thrilled to have executed this Formula 1 design using SolidWorks and rendered it with KeyShot. Automotive engineering has always been a passion of mine, even as a child when I had little understanding of the complexities behind it. High-performance motorsport, particularly Formula 1, has always fascinated me. It represents the pinnacle of automotive technology—akin to space expeditions, but for cars—where groundbreaking research and innovation constantly push the boundaries of what's possible.

This project marks my first Formula 1 design using SolidWorks, and I’m incredibly proud of how it turned out.

What’s Next?

Moving forward, I plan to simulate computational fluid dynamics (CFD) in my next project to explore the aerodynamic potential of this design.

Skills:

  • SOLIDWORKS: Used for creating the 3D design of the Formula 1 car.
  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD): Tools and techniques used to construct the model.
  • 3D Rendering: Rendered with KeyShot for realistic visualization.
  • Engineering Design: Application of mechanical engineering principles to develop the Formula 1 model.
