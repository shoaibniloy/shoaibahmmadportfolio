AI Audio Smart Recycling Bin

Overview:

A mechatronic recycling bin that classifies items by their impact sound, then opens a servo-driven trapdoor and rotates a NEMA-17 stepper-powered carousel to drop the item into the correct one of four compartments. The structure uses 2020 aluminum extrusion, clear cylindrical panels, and fluorescent acrylic lids/doors for safety and visibility.

  • Audio ML on edge: Mic + TinyML model (Nicla Voice / XIAO nRF52840 Sense / Nano 33 BLE Sense) infer can / PET bottle / paper / other.
  • Controller: Custom “AI Audio Classifier” board with Arduino Pro Portenta C33, TMC2208 stepper driver, 5V buck, servo and hall interfaces.
  • Indexing: A3144 Hall sensor + magnet for homing/slot detection; GT2 belt + 28T pulley drive.
  • UX & Safety: Enclosed funnel and acrylic guards; printed feet; removable top for service.

What’s Next?

Improve robustness in the wild: add a TOF/IR gate to prevent multi-drop, implement bin-full sensing (ultrasonic or load cells), servo soft-close profiles, additional index magnets per bin, and telemetry to a cloud dashboard (counts, jams, voltage, maintenance).

Skills:

  • CAD & Fabrication: 2020 extrusion design, laser-cut acrylic, 3D-printed parts.
  • Embedded & Electronics: Portenta C33, TMC2208, servo control, Hall sensing, buck/charging, battery power (3S LiPo).
  • TinyML Audio: MFCC/log-mel features, compact CNN/DS-CNN on impact-triggered buffers, dataset curation & evaluation.
  • Mechatronics: Belt-driven indexing carousel, jam handling, homing routines, state-machine firmware.
  • IoT: Wi-Fi connectivity, multi-node topology, cloud dashboarding for analytics and alerts.
