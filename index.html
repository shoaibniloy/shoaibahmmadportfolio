About Me

About Me

I’m a Mechanical Engineering graduate with a strong focus on the fusion of AI, machine learning, and automation within robotics and mechanical applications. From my undergraduate days, I’ve been driven by a passion for self-learning, constantly honing my skills to stay at the forefront of technological advancements. I tried to fulfill my thirst for knowledge from various online courses which have been crucial in sharpening my expertise and equipping me with the tools to translate emerging technologies into real-world solutions. Throughout my academic journey, I’ve had the privilege of working on innovative projects such as Line Following Robots, Robotic Arms, and Soccer Bots. My participation in numerous competitions has led to several accolades, including being a finalist at Hult Prize RUET 2023 for my AI-powered smart recycle bin, which garnered national TV attention. I’ve also secured 1st Runner-Up positions in the Line Follower Robot (LFR) Segment at EEE Day KUET 2023, IUT ESONANCE 2023, and RUET EEE Day 2023, as well as reaching the finals of the SDG Policy Challenge among only five national teams. In addition to my hands-on projects, I’ve contributed to five research projects, where I’ve deepened my understanding of AI integration in mechanical engineering and contibution to scientific community. My ultimate goal is to advance cutting-edge research in AI and machine learning, applying them to the mechanical and mechatronics fields to create innovative, impactful solutions for the future.
