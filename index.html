About Me

About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineering graduate from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology, Bangladesh, with a BSc. in Mechanical Engineering and a CGPA of 2.92/4.00, expected to graduate in April 2025. My passion lies in the fusion of AI, machine learning, and automation within robotics and mechanical applications. Through self-learning and various online courses, I’ve honed my expertise in robotics and control systems, including ROS, ROS2, Gazebo, AirSim, PX4, Ardupilot, MAVLink, sensor fusion, and autonomous navigation, as well as reinforcement learning techniques like PPO, SAC, and DQN. My skills extend to computer vision with tools such as YOLO, DeepSORT, SLAM, and Vision Language Models, alongside predictive learning methods like Gaussian Process Regression and Bayesian optimization. I am proficient in programming languages like Python, C++, and Java, and engineering design software including SolidWorks, Fusion 360, and ANSYS. I’ve worked on innovative projects such as a handmade quadcopter, Line Following Robots, Soccer Bots, a 4DoF Robotic Arm, and advanced drone perception systems like Zenitsu V1. My research experience includes contributing to five research projects, with under-review articles such as *Autonomous Navigation of Low-Cost Quadcopters in Confined Spaces Using Multi-Modal Perception, PPO Control, and LLM-Driven Semantic Reasoning* and *Development of a Predictive Model for Material Selection in Impact Protector Design Using Gaussian Process Regression*. In competitions, I’ve earned accolades like being a finalist at the Hult Prize RUET 2023 for my AI-powered smart recycle bin, which gained national TV attention, and securing 1st Runner-Up in the Line Follower Robot segment at EEE Day KUET 2023, IUT ESONANCE 2023, and RUET EEE Day 2023. My goal is to advance cutting-edge research in AI and machine learning, applying them to mechanical and mechatronics fields to create impactful solutions.
