Overview:

Excited to unveil my latest project—a turbofan engine model crafted in SOLIDWORKS and rendered with KeyShot. This design combines technical precision and innovative tools to explore the complexities of aerospace engineering.

I will conduct a CFD simulation to analyze and enhance airflow dynamics, pushing this design closer to real-world standards.
