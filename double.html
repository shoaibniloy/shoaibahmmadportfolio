Soccerbot Project

Overview:

This soccer robot is built for robotics competitions, combining innovative design with custom hardware for smooth and efficient gameplay. It uses a hand-crafted controller and Bluetooth connectivity to enhance gameplay performance.

Components and Construction:

  • Motors: Powered by four high-torque motors for precise control and fast acceleration, ensuring smooth turns and quick responses.
  • Chassis Design: Designed with Autodesk Fusion 360, focusing on durability and lightweight construction, built to withstand aggressive maneuvers during competitive matches.
  • Control System: The robot features a Bluetooth module for wireless communication, enabling low-latency control and quick reactions.

Functionality:

  • Movement: The robot’s four high-torque motors enable precise and fast movements, allowing it to make sharp turns and accelerate quickly on the field.
  • Wireless Control: The Bluetooth-enabled controller provides direct, responsive control over the robot’s actions, ensuring seamless gameplay experience.

Custom Controller:

  • Handmade Circuit: The custom controller features a hand-soldered circuit on a perforated board, ensuring high reliability and precise control.
  • Bluetooth Connectivity: Integrated Bluetooth module enables wireless communication with the robot, ensuring uninterrupted control during matches.

Personalization:

This project overcomes local resource limitations through the hand-soldered circuit design and custom-built controller, showcasing my technical skill, problem-solving, and creativity in the competitive robotics space.

Achievements:

Designed to compete at the highest level, this soccerbot demonstrates cutting-edge design and functionality for robotics competitions.
