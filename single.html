Line Follower Robot

Advanced Line Follower Robot Project

Overview:

Designed and built a high-precision Line Follower Robot using an Arduino Mega, capable of following lines and navigating walls with exceptional accuracy.

Components and Construction:

  • Microcontroller: Arduino Mega for advanced processing and control.
  • Sensors:
    • Eight line-detection sensors for precise path tracking.
    • Three ultrasonic sensors for accurate wall detection and navigation.
  • Circuit Design: Hand-soldered on a veroboard due to the lack of PCB services in Bangladesh, showcasing resourcefulness and technical skill.
  • Wheels: Custom 3D-printed wheels, designed to meet specific size requirements unavailable in the local market.

Functionality:

  • Line Following: Utilizes eight sensors to detect and follow lines with high precision.
  • Wall Following: Employs three ultrasonic sensors to detect walls and adjust the robot’s path dynamically.
